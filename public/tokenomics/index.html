MetEast

White Paper

What

MetEast – a decentralized NFT marketplace on Elastos·ESC, with better liquidity, autonomous governance and more friendly interaction. In MetEast, you can mint and trade NFTs through encryption assets agreements. We aim to solve the liquidity problem of the piling-up NFTs for all users and provide token rewards for both creators and traders.

Why

Till now, tens of millions of NFTs have been minted. However, most of them are no longer touched once minted and vested in numerous wallets of the users with problems of circulation and monetization. MetEast visions to change and sort out these issues. By our unique mechanism, MetEast will help all the users tackle the problem of poor liquidity of piling-up NFTs.

How

  • All users can mint their own NFT mystery boxes: the users will decide whatever NFTs to put into the mystery boxes, eg. a combination of rare, valuable, popular and easy-to-sell NFTs with less so NFTs to make sure the potential buyers can get high-value NFTs at low cost by certain chance;
  • For the less lucky users missing the chance, there will still be trading rewards for buying NFT mystery box;
  • The NFT mystery box minted on MetEast through smart contracts constitute a NFT itself, which means once minted it cannot be revised or revoked. For the buyers, mystery boxes in NFT leaving no place for cheating means fair game.
  • We will deploy on ETH as well, which means most of the NFTs on Opensea can be circulated to MetEast.

MetEast Features:

For our ambition and solutions to tackle the aforementioned problems, MetEast will include the following features in the first phase:

  • NFT minting: users can mint their own NFTs on MetEast or circulate their NFTs on Opensea and other NFT platform to MetEast.
  • NFT mystery boxes minting: in MetEast 1.0, we provide the stability and compatibility of up to 100 NFTs in one NFT mystery box by the users.
  • Trading rewards: users are provided with ME Coin as rewards for trading.
  • DAO: we will establish the community governance on DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization). Tokens released by MetEast are not only crypto valuable tokens but also ballot tickets.

In the second phase, we will launch Defi and credit systems:

  • Deploying on ETH
  • Defi: NFT staking and loan
  • Credit: NFT combined with digital ID to relise anonymous credit assessment.

MetEast Token --ME COIN

The ME COIN awards system is specially designed for trading as mining. The users can get ME COINs by trading and minting to trade, etc. The total supply of ME COIN will be 1,000,000,000, 500,000,000 of which will be allocated in ESC ecosystem and 500,000,000 of which will be allocated in ETH ecosystem.

MetEast intends to be a value shared NFT platform. The more valuable MetEast as a platform, the more valuable ME COINs will be and it will be more worthy for the users to hold more ME COINs.

Release Schedule of ME COINs

The detailed rules of awards to the users are as follows:

  • Minter (creator) can claim ME COINs as awards calculated by = (ME COINs released per day / Total transaction volume per day) * their minted NFT(s) transaction volume per day * 0.4
  • Trader can claim ME COINs as awards calculated by = ((ME COINs released per day / Total transaction volume per day) * their traded NFT(s) transaction volume per day * 0.5
  • DAOs as minter (creator) can claim extra awards calculated by = (ME COINs released per day/Total transaction volume per day) * their minted NFT(s) transaction volume per day * 0.1
  • DAOs as trader can claim extra awards calculated by = (ME COINs released per day/Total transaction volume per day) * their traded NFT(s) transaction volume per day * 0.1
